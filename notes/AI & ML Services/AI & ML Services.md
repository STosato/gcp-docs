# AI & ML Services
[AI Building Blocks](<./AI Building Blocks.md>) provide AI through simple REST calls  
[[Cloud AutoML]] enables training models on custom datasets  
[[AI Platform]] provides end-to-end ML pipelines on-premises and cloud
